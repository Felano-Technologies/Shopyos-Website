How to Delete Your Account

Important Information:

Deleting your account is permanent. Once your account is deleted, all your data, including order history, saved addresses, and payment methods, will be permanently removed from our systems. This action cannot be undone.

1 Via the Shopyos Mobile App

person

Step 1: Open Profile

Open the Shopyos app and tap the Profile icon in the bottom right corner of the navigation bar.

settings

Step 2: Access Settings

Tap on the Settings gear icon located at the top right of your profile page.

manage_accounts

Step 3: Account Management

Select Account Management from the settings menu.

delete_forever

Step 4: Request Deletion

Tap on Delete Account. You will be asked to provide a reason for leaving (optional).

check_circle

Step 5: Final Confirmation

Read the final warning and tap Confirm Deletion. Your account will be scheduled for immediate removal.

2 Via Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team at support@shopyos.com. Please use the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and send the email from the address associated with your Shopyos account.

3 What Data Is Deleted & What Is Kept

delete_forever

Deleted Immediately

  • close Profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • close Saved addresses and delivery locations
  • close Payment methods and wallet information
  • close Profile photos and uploaded images
  • close Chat messages and communication history
  • close Preferences and app settings
schedule

Deleted Within 90 Days

  • schedule Order history and transaction records
  • schedule Product listings (for Sellers)
  • schedule Delivery records (for Drivers)
  • schedule Reviews and ratings you have given or received
gavel

Retained for Legal Purposes

Some data may be retained beyond 90 days where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • info Financial transaction records (as required by tax and financial regulations)
  • info Fraud prevention and security logs
  • info Dispute resolution records

Note: For security reasons, we may require you to verify your identity before processing an email deletion request. This process usually takes 3-5 business days. Once deletion is confirmed, it cannot be reversed.
